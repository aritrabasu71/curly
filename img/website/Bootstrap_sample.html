About

Purbayan Chatterjee, an eminent drummer and musician residing in Kolkata, has worked with a lot of musicians in the recent years. He is currently playing with a band and is also a founding member of the band. Through this website he is trying to communicate with all the drum enthusiasts out there and he also provides course materials for learning basic and advanced drumming.
