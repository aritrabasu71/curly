About Us

From where curly came

Hello there
Welcome to Curly! I’m a Software Engineer who fell in love with my curly hair around 2019. I still remember the days when I would comb and brush my hair over and over again expecting miracles to happen, but my hair forever looked like hay. And now after umpteen experiments with techniques, hair products and methods, my hair hasn’t looked better! I was already writing beauty articles for other bloggers, but as a curly haired girl I saw few beauty bloggers in India writing about curls. Part of the idea behind curlsandbeautydiary is to make all you curly haired beauties fall in love with your curly hair.

My roots are in God’s own country, Kerala, a beautiful tiny state lying on the tail of India. I stay Bangalore, married to the love of my life.

I love to eat and backpack. And being a typical girly girl, I love beauty products, a little bit of makeup, perfumes, jewelry, accessories and beautiful clothes. All of this whizzed up in a blender – Curls and Beauty Diary.

XOXO! Gautami Sinha

My Skin type – Combination, Acne prone My Hair type – 2c/3a curl pattern, Medium porosity, Medium density, Coarse
